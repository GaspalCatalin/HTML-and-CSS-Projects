Sedona, Arizona (USA)

Have you always imagined a spectacular sunrise or a romantic sunset with a breathtaking view? Well, (the) Red Rocks of Sedona will be your ultimate escape from your super busy city. The vibrancy of the colors and the simplicity of the Sedona landscape will bring you the peace and quiet you desire. Maybe you just want to contemplate the wonderful gift of nature or maybe you want to walk or maybe even climb. We have the best opportunities for you!

Saronic Gulf, Athens (Greece)

The blue horizon, where it merges in a sublime way, as if in perfect harmony, the sky and the earth. This is the panorama that will strike you in the Saronic Gulf. It is no coincidence that Greece is considered the land of the gods, where gods and men share the same feelings and customs, pleasant or spicy. Trust us, you will be hit with the same dreamy feeling that will make you evaporate into a land of legend, while delicious food will bring you back down to earth.
