W. Shakespeare

W. Shakespeare is considered the greatest writer in the English language. While his original work is considered to be "in the Public Domain", any creation based on it can be protected by copyright. Always pay attention to © mark. We reproduce here one of the most famous quotes from his work:

To be, or not to be: that is the question:
Whether 'tis nobler in the mind to suffer
The slings and arrows of outrageous fortune,
Or to take arms against a sea of troubles,
And by opposing end them? To die: to sleep;
(Hamlet, Act 1 Scene 5)

Shakespeare's Birthplace Museum

Visit Shakespeare’s Birthplace – William Shakespeare’s childhood home in the heart of Stratford-upon-Avon. Hear tales of Shakespeare’s family life and get up close to objects from the Trust’s world class collections as you discover how the extraordinary playwright continues to shape our lives today.

Opening hours: from 10:00 to 16:00 everyday.
A perfect place for a date on Valentines day.

Romanian writers

M. Eminescu

M. Eminescu is generally regarded as the most famous and influential Romanian poet. While his original work is considered to be "in the Public Domain", any creation based on it can be protected by copyright. Always pay attention to © mark. We reproduce here one of the most famous quotes from his work:

A fost odată ca-n povești,
A fost ca niciodată.
Din rude mari împărătești,
O prea frumoasă fată.
(Luceafărul)

Mihai Eminescu Museum

The Museum was inaugurated in 1989 and here you can find a documentary exhibition in honour of Mihai Eminescu, the national poet. You can see books, documents, photographs, texts, 62 photocopies of Mihai Eminescu's manuscripts, original photographs, painting and a sculpture collection.

Opening hours: from 10:00 to 17:00 everyday.
A perfect place for a date on Eminescu's Birthday.
